# 🚴 Bike Sales Data Analysis Project

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing bike sales data to uncover patterns, trends, and key factors influencing customer purchasing behavior. The dataset includes demographic, socioeconomic, and lifestyle variables to help understand what drives bike purchases.

## ❗ Problem Statement
The dataset provides detailed information about customers, including:
- Age, Gender, Marital Status  
- Income, Education, Occupation  
- Commute Distance, Region  

However, it does not directly explain **why customers purchase bikes**.

👉 The goal is to:
- Identify patterns in purchasing behavior  
- Discover key influencing factors  
- Help businesses improve marketing strategies and customer targeting  

---

## 🔍 Research Questions

### 1. Demographic Factors
- How does age influence bike purchases?  
- Are there gender-based differences?  
- Does marital status impact buying behavior?  

### 2. Socioeconomic Factors
- How does income affect bike purchases?  
- What is the impact of education level?  
- Which occupations buy more bikes?  

### 3. Lifestyle Factors
- How does commute distance influence decisions?  
- Does home ownership matter?  
- How does car ownership affect bike purchases?  

### 4. Regional Insights
- Are there regional differences in bike purchases?  
- Do cultural habits affect buying trends?  

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- 🚀 **Middle-aged individuals (31–54)** are the highest buyers  
- 💰 Higher income increases likelihood of purchase  
- 🚶 Short (0–1 miles) & moderate (5–10 miles) commute distances dominate  
- 🚗 Fewer cars → Higher bike purchase probability  
- 👔 Professionals & managers purchase more bikes  
- 🏠 Homeowners are more likely to buy bikes  

---

## 📈 Detailed Analysis Highlights

### 👥 Demographics
- Balanced gender distribution  
- Singles tend to purchase more than married individuals  

### 💼 Socioeconomic Trends
- Bachelor's degree holders are top buyers  
- Professionals, managers, clerical workers lead purchases  

### 🚴 Lifestyle Behavior
- Short-distance commuters prefer bikes  
- Fewer vehicles → higher bike adoption  

### 🌍 Regional Trends
- Europe → Short commute usage  
- North America → Medium commute buyers  
- Pacific → Mid-range commuters

## 🎯 Business Recommendations

### 🎯 Target Segments
